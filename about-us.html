About Us

FUTURE

The school is one of the official bodies in the society, which has the function of raising children and working to raise their abilities and skills in various fields, working alongside the family in the socialization of the individual and the planting of human values.

School functions

 The function of the school is not only to teach students some scientific sciences and knowledge, but also to function more than the following: Cultural transfer, where the school works to transfer the cultural heritage inherited to the new generation in an easy and easy way after its revision and disinfection of myths that were Stuck to it. Social integration, because the community contains many different groups, the role of the school, to remove the contradictions that may exist between these groups, and integrate them. The personal development of the school student is nurtured within and outside its boundaries, by creating a strong and cohesive personality. Develop new behavioral and social patterns in the individual and work on their development on scientific and cognitive bases so that the student can adapt to the society in which he lives. The development of creative abilities, the school is working on the development of scientific capacity, and sponsors creative ideas, and develop the student curiosity of knowledge that drives him to succeed. Providing the appropriate atmosphere that encourages the student to exercise his democratic right and his human relationship in school and beyond.

Our vision

Graduates with an efficient armed with scientific creativity and ideals capable of keeping up with the changes of the age.
